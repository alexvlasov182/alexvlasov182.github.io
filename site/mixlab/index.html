Patients first, always

We know how stressful it can be when your pet needs meds. That’s why we offer free next-day delivery and even do same-day in NYC and LA. We got you.

Tailored care

We’ll work with you and your veterinarian to find the very best solution for your pet. With our custom compounded options, we can change the dosage, flavor and form. We even have a flavor guarantee with over 40 options!

How it works

We receive your prescription

Either your veterinarian sends us a prescription or we reach out to them for you. Whatever is easiest!

We deliver your care package

Fast and free next-day delivery, straight to your door. Same-day in NYC and LA.

You treat your pet

Give your pet the care they need to feel better. Plus they can play with our included toy!
